Contact-Free Vital Signs Monitoring
Using Millimeter Wave Radar

Made Keeping You In Mind.

Keeping track of your vital signs is important. That's why, using a 60 GHz radar, we can accurately and contactlessly measure your heart rate, breathing rate, and heart rate variability.

Only The Best Components For You.

Acconeer XM112

This module is a state of the art one.

Raspberry Pi 3

It is used for on-board DSP and to transmit rersults to the database.

What Makes BioRadar Different?

Internet of Medical Things

Our project has been designed in accordance with the growing trend towards the Internet of Things. The data is securely stored in a realtime database.

High Accuracy

Through the on-board real time signal processing, we have managed to obtain an accuracy of greater than 90% when compared with an ECG sensor.

Privacy

Through the Acconeer A1 radar, we are able to measure your vital signs without having to store or use any personal data of you like photos or videos.

What's Happening Behind the Scenes?

Obtain Data

The Acconner module is used to get data from the patient continously.

Process Data

The data is sent to the Raspberry Pi where on-borad, real times DSP is performed and vital signs are extracted.

Store and Display Data

The data is then sent periodicly to the realtime database to be stored, and the website then retreieves and displays the data in realtime.
